Hello. I'm Jona.

Graphic and Web Designer

I am a graphic designer, calligrapher, and web designer based in North Carolina. I have worked professionally as a designer for over six years. As a designer, my creative style is noticeable by the use of negative space and typographical hierarchy. I believe in providing content that matters without the extra unnecessary components.

I have created a blog to assist fellow creatives with tutorials, tips, and career advice. Check out my blog at heyishottheserif.com
